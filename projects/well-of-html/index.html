Pokemon Go is a free-to-play, location-based augmented reality game developed by Niantic for iOS and Android devices. The game was the result of a collaboration between Niantic and Nintendo, by way of The Pokemon Company, and was initially released in selected countries in July 2016. The game utilizes the player's mobile device's GPS ability to locate, capture, battle, and train virtual creatures, called Pokemon, which appear on the screen as if they were at the same real-world location as the player. The game features a freemium business model and supports in-app purchases for additional in-game items.

PokeWorkout? Yes! Workout!

Pokemon Go is a great way to workout while having fun. You hatch eggs to get pokemon by walking. The more you walk the more eggs you hatch. Eggs have a diffrent amount of kilometers needed to hatch. 2km eggs hatch common pokemon. 5km eggs hatch rarer pokemon. 10km hatch rare pokemon. They are usually the most powerful.

Gyms

PokeGyms are real life locations that in-game can be controled by a team. To obtain controll of a gym you must defeat the pokemon there to defend it. Once you have defeated their pokemon you can take over the gym. A gym that has been taken over now refelects your team's color and other members of your team can add to the gym.

Raids

Raids are one of the newest additions to Pokemon Go. You can obtain powerful pokemon thru raids. During a raid you and a group of your friends defeat the pokemon and then you have a chance to catch it.
